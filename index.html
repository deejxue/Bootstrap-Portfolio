Meet Alex...

Alex is a passionate, budding web developer with a love of technology. For years he's been supporting friends, family and clients with solutions for their technical problems. From mobile devices to setting up home networks to training users on new platforms and applications, Alex is the goto guy.


But make no mistake, Alex is not all work and no play. He knows how to have fun--from diving, dragon boating, skiing, surfing, to enjoying quiet nights reading at his abode on the East, Alex is deeply passionate about life. In a previous life, Alex even played a little defensive end on the gridiron.

He has been involved with non-profits helping out the community, political movements to help the under-presented and is always looking for how to integrate tech with social progress. No stranger to the ups and downs of life, he strives to help others who lack the knowledge and experience with tech so that they can have the benefits of technological progress.


Presently Alex is doing full stack web developement and has exciting projects on the horizon. If you have an idea or problem you're looking to resolve with a technology solution, Alex is your guy. Why not drop him a line and explore a possible collaboration to resolve your own tech obstacles?
